The Bougain Villa Information

Bedroom #1

The master bedroom features a queen sized bed, a dresser, a desk, and board games for you to enjoy. This Bedroom also connects to the bathroom which has a the toilet, a shower and a sink.

Bedroom #2

The second bedroom features two twin sized beds and is also the home to the washing machine and dryer. This bedroom is also connected to the backyard.

The Backyard

Behind the house is a small yard with many chairs to relax in and a charcoal grill.

Living Room

In the living room there are two comfortable couches to sit on. There is also a small TV connected to a chromecast, so you can access Netlix, Hulu, Disney+, etc.

Kitchen

The kitchen has an oven, refridgerator, microwave, and a sink. There is also a dining table with four chairs.
