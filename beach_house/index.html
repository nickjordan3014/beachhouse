The Bougain Villa in Ormond by the Sea

The House

The Bougain Villa is a small house that comfortably sleeps four people. The house is located walking distance from the beach, so you don't have to worry about finding beach parking. Around the house, there are many tourist attractions to explore and restaurants to eat at.

Location

34 River Drive, Ormond Beach, FL

Price

$75 a night + $75 cleaning fee
